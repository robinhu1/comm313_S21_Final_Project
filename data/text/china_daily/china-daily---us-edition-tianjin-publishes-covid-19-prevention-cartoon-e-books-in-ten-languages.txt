


Tianjin publishes COVID-19 prevention cartoon e-books in ten languages

The Tianjin government has promoted the publication of a cartoon e-book guide to COVID-19 prevention and control. The guide, published in ten languages, is an effort to facilitate the fight against the virus both at home and abroad.
The e-books are available in English, Spanish, Portuguese, German, Korean, French, Italian, Japanese, Arabic, and Russian.
The cartoons not only provide details of scientific and effective protections against the virus, but also make the e-books easy and pleasurable to read.
The different language versions also reflect the traditions and lifestyles of their countries of origin, to serve as a popular and pleasurable tool.
They are published by Tianjin Publishing and Media Group under the guidance of the Tianjin Municipal Information Office.
